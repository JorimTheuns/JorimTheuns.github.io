Dissolving Dichotomies

Welcome.

This is the online portal to an experimental research and design project by:

  • Ceola Tunstall-Behrens (MA Contextual Design)
  • Corto Jabali (MA Social Design)
  • Jorim Theuns (MA Information Design)

Master students at Design Academy Eindhoven.

Research: Looking up Outcome: Homo Tempus

This project blurs and dissolves. Deconstruction through construction: Telling truth through lies.

We recognise, through our past experience: the strength of science and the humanities, done diligently, in destabilising the regimes of truth by which we live; our rigid ways of thinking.

As design students (lacking the diligence to do good science], we have chosen to impart past wisdom by means of fable.

We hijack the sciences to impart a philosophy. One which, if taken seriously, could dissolve unjustly held distinctions between the Same and the Other; the borders in our heads.
