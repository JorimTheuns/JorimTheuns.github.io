Frames of Reference

Frames of Reference is a 1960 educational film about physics. It explains the distinction between inertial and nonintertial frames of reference using humorous camera tricks. It highlights how our personal frame of reference controls what we perceive and experience.

The Law of Gravitation

In his lectures on gravitational physics Richard Feynman simplifies Newtonian and Einsteinian physics for a crowd of non scientists. His use of metaphors and drawings to explain unintiutive and complex question about our physical reality makes these lectures and invaluable resource of understanding.
