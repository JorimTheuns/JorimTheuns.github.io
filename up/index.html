What?

This website documents the research behind an experimental design project by students @ Design Academy Eindhoven.

Theme?

Designers at the border.

Brief?

Look Up.

We explored borders from earth to space, and investigated and questioned their physical, material and immaterial natures. This website shares findings, outputs and concepts from the research and contains a bibliography where you can find relevant resources. This is design research at the border between space and time, pasts and presents.
